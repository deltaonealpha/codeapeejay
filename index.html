    Gaming

    Domain: 9-12

    No. of participants: 2

  • Due to CODE being an online event, participants will evidently use their own equipment. We will not be responsible for potato hardware, crippling Windows updates or lags/ high ping in any way.
  • Brawlhalla: 2v2 round, two schools competing each other. Winning team gets qualified to the next round. Loser team will compete each other for further qualification. If the number of losing teams are odd, then their scores will be calculated. Team with higher score will get qualified to next round and other two teams will compete for the same.
  • Krunker: It will be of two round each limiting for 5 minutes. The first round will be the sniper only round where both the competing teams have to use only sniper rifles and no other choice of weaponry to eliminate the competition. The winning team will get qualified to the next round and the losing team will get disqualified.
  • Killer Instincts/Fantasy strike: One of the members from each school will be competing in this round against each other. It will be a simple game of fight off with 2 rounds(3 if the two rounds are tied). The team who gets K.O> in the final round disqualifies and the opponent wins and qualifies to the next round.
  • Trackmania: Same as the fighting genre, in this game each school will send one of its candidate but this time he/she won’t be competing anyone but will be playing solos. Each schools timing which it took them to finish the track will be noted down and the top 2 schools will compete in the finale.
  • RIOT VALORANT: The finals will be a single 3v3 custom UNRATED match with purchasable weapons and abilities and disabled cheats. Finals with Valorant will last around 40 minutes.
  • • Each and every participant needs to join the discord server for the event for the communication purposes.
  • • Any form of cheating to be found would straight away lead to the disqualification of the school.
  • • Use of controllers is allowed for all the games that supports a controller.
  • Players should have a strong and consistent internet connection. Typical gamer excuses like lag will not be considered until we observe an absurdly high ping ourselves
